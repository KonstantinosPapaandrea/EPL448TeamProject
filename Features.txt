Future columns:
out_prncp: the remaining outstanding principal on the loan right now (how much principal is still unpaid)
out_prncp_inv: the remaining outstanding principal attributed to investors’ portion of the loan (same idea as out_prncp, but for the investor-funded share)
total_pymnt: the total amount of money received from the borrower so far, combining principal + interest + fees (i.e., “all payments to date”)
total_pymnt_inv: total payments credited to investors to date (the investor-side share of total_pymnt)
total_rec_prncp: the total principal that has been repaid/received to date
total_rec_int: the total interest that has been received to date
total_rec_late_fee: total late fees that have been received to date
recoveries: money recovered after the loan has been charged off, typically
collection_recovery_fee: fees charged for the recovery/collection process (the cost associated with collecting recoveries)
last_pymnt_amnt: the amount of the most recent payment made by the borrower.
last_pymnt_d: the date the most recent payment was received on the loan (a “last payment date” timestamp)
next_pymnt_d: the next scheduled payment due date (what the system expects as the upcoming payment date, based on the current repayment schedule/status). 
last_credit_pull_d: the most recent date LendingClub pulled the borrower’s credit report
last_fico_range_low: the lower bound of the borrower’s latest FICO score band at the time of the most recent update/pull
last_fico_range_high: the upper bound of the borrower’s latest FICO score band at the time of the most recent update/pull
Hardship is something that happens later when the borrower can't meet the original schedule.
hardship_flag, orig_projected_additional_accrued_interest, hardship_type, hardship_reason, hardship_status, deferral_term, hardship_amount, hardship_start_date, hardship_end_date, payment_plan_start_date, hardship_length, hardship_dpd, hardship_loan_status, hardship_payoff_balance_amount, hardship_last_payment_amount
Dept_settlement:settlement is late-stage resolution process so these exist after serious charge-off paths
ebt_settlement_flag, debt_settlement_flag_date, settlement_status, settlement_date, settlement_amount, settlement_percentage, settlement_term
Identifiers:(No point in using them might lead to memorization effects)
id, member_id, url
Borderline:(Exactly when the loan happens)
funded_amnt,funded_amnt_inv
Safe features:

loan_amnt: the loan amount the borrower requested (principal at origination).

term: the repayment length of the loan (typically 36 or 60 months).

int_rate: the interest rate charged on the loan.

installment: the scheduled monthly payment amount (based on amount, rate, term).

grade: LendingClub’s overall credit grade assigned to the loan (risk tier).

sub_grade: a more granular grade within grade (finer risk tier).

home_ownership: borrower’s housing status (e.g., RENT/OWN/MORTGAGE).

verification_status: whether the borrower’s income was verified (e.g., Verified / Source Verified / Not Verified).

issue_d: the month/year the loan was issued/funded (origination date).

purpose: the borrower-selected reason for the loan (debt_consolidation, credit_card, etc.).

addr_state: borrower’s state of residence.

fico_range_low: lower bound of the borrower’s FICO score band at origination.

fico_range_high: upper bound of the borrower’s FICO score band at origination.

revol_bal: borrower’s total revolving credit balance (e.g., credit cards) on the credit report snapshot.

revol_util: revolving utilization rate = revolving balance / revolving credit limit (how “maxed out” revolving credit is).

dti: debt-to-income ratio (borrower’s monthly debt payments relative to income) at the time of application/origination snapshot.

annual_inc: borrower’s self-reported annual income.

emp_length: borrower’s length of employment (tenure).

emp_title: borrower’s job title (free text).

earliest_cr_line: date of the borrower’s oldest credit line (how long they’ve had credit history).

Credit-history counters (from the credit report snapshot; they measure past credit behavior, not future loan performance):

delinq_2yrs: number of 30+ day delinquencies in the past 2 years.

inq_last_6mths: number of credit inquiries in the last 6 months (often a proxy for recent credit-seeking).

open_acc: number of open credit accounts/lines.

total_acc: total number of credit accounts in the borrower’s file (open + closed).

pub_rec: number of derogatory public records (e.g., certain legal/financial public events).

tax_liens: number of tax liens.

collections_12_mths_ex_med: number of collections in last 12 months, excluding medical collections.

chargeoff_within_12_mths: number of charge-offs in the last 12 months (accounts written off as losses).
